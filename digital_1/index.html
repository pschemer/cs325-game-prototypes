  • High Concept: Nutrition Nut! helps you better recognize categories of foods.
  • Theme: Learning, early development.
  • Mandated Variety: Keyboard input (S, F, V, P), Randomly chosen categories and items, 2-D style.
  • Prototype Goal: Matching user input to a randomly chosen item in a table.
  • Player Experience Goals: Learning basic nutrition.
  • Gameplay: A randomly chosen food item is displayed at the center of the screen. The player must match the food to a category. Categories are Fruit, Vegetable, Protein, and Sweets. A category is chosen by pressing the corresponding key: F for Fruit, V for Vegetable, P for Protein, and S for Sweet. The player is notified of the correctness of their decision. The score is incremented on success or reset to zero on fail. If the player reaches 10 pointes, they're rewarded with a special prize and the game is complete.
  • Strategies: What player strategies do you expect will be effective at playing this game?
    • Memory / Mental Fortitude
  • Story/Setting/Premise: This is not a story driven game.
  • Target Audience: Young children.
  • Play Time: 1-2 minutes.
